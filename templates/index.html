Rice Disease Prevention Guide

Essential knowledge for every rice farmer

Prevention Methods
Use Resistant Varieties

Plant disease-resistant rice varieties suitable for your region.

Proper Water Management

Avoid continuous flooding and ensure proper drainage.

Balanced Fertilization

Avoid excessive nitrogen which can increase disease susceptibility.

Field Sanitation

Remove infected plant debris and practice crop rotation.

Early Detection Signs
Leaf Discoloration

Look for yellowing, browning, or unusual spots on leaves.

Abnormal Growth

Watch for stunted growth or wilting plants.

Lesion Patterns

Note the shape, size, and color of lesions on leaves.

Spread Pattern

Monitor how quickly symptoms spread across the field.
